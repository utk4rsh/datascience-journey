
One more suspect in the July 11 serial train bomb blasts has been arrested,
Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) sources
said.

 Mohammed Naved was
arrested from Hyderabad on Friday and will be produced in a local court on
Saturday, the sources
said.

 This is the 15th arrest
made by ATS in connection with the July 11 blasts which killed nearly 200 people
and injured over 500.
