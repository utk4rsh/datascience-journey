 Explosive batsman Yuvraj Singh
on Sunday consulted an orthopedic surgeon after feeling pain in the little
finger of his left hand.

 India and Sri Lanka teams arrived here on
Sunday for the first One-Day International of the five-match series, beginning
on Tuesday. 

 The teams arrived here on a chartered plane amidst tight
security. 

 Yuvraj was taken to Dr Dinkar Virparia's clinic for
check-up after the left-hand batsman complained of pain in his finger, sources
in the Saurashtra Cricket Association said.

 Later he was taken to the
Rajkot Imaging Centre where an X-ray of his finger was taken.
