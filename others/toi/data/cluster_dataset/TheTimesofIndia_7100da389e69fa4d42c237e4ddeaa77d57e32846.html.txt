 A delegation led by MNS
leaders met Maharashtra Governor S C Jamir today, requesting 'Z' category
security to MNS chief Raj Thackeray.

 "We have requested to give 'Z'
category security to Raj Thackeray, in the wake of continuous threats to him,
"MNS leader Shishir Shinde who led the delegation said.

 Raj's
security was withdrawn by the State Government, he said to the Governor and
demanded him to direct the State Government to restore the
security.

 "It should be the endeavour of political parties to respect
the sentiments of every citizen and maintain the unity and integrity of the
country," the Governor said to the delegation.

 He also assured them
that he will bring the issue to the notice of the State Government.
