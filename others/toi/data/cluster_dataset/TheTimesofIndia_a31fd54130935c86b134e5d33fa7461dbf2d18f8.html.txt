 Ten Lok Sabha members,
caught on camera accepting money for raising questions, were on Friday expelled
from the House after BJP and some of its allies walked out.

 The 10
members are Annasahed M K Patil, Y G Mahajan, Suresh Chandel, Pradeep Gandhi and
Chandra Pratap Singh (all BJP), Narendra Kumar Kushwaha, Lal Chandra Kol and
Rajaram Pal (all BSP), Manoj Kumar (RJD) and Ramsevak Singh (Cong) who were
caught on camera accepting money for raising questions in the House.
