
 26/11 is not a day we would want to
remember. But remember we must. For the 170 innocent lives that were so wantonly
taken, and for the fact that it was an assault on the soul of India.


 We've seen these pictures, some over and over again. There were many
more that we didn't publish then - and even a year later, we couldn't bring
ourselves to publish them. 

 Indian philosophy teaches us to move on,
to not dwell on the past. But much as we may try, certain things are difficult
to forget...
