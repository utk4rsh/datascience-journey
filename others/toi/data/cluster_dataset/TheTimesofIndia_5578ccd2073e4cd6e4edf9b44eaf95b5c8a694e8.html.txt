
 The big hitting all-rounder is a key
member of the New Zealand side.

 Jacob Oram is one of just 6 New
Zealanders to have reached the double of 1000 ODI runs and 100
wickets.

 Oram has 3 Test centuries to his credit and he also held the
record of the fastest ever ODI century by a New Zealander, the fastest ever made
against Australia, when he smashed an unbeaten 101 off 72 balls.
